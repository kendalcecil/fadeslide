Traveling around

Here's a couple of fun spots I've gotten to see!

Pittsburgh,PA

I was raised in Pittsburgh Pennslyvania from 1st grade through my early 20's. Fun-fact: It's named one of the top 10 cloudiest cities in America.

West Virginia

Country roads, take me home! It's easy to fall in love with West Virgina; filled with funny monster themed tourist traps, to old American history throughout the streets. It's by far some of the most relaxing vacations I've ever taken.

Colorado

My most recent adventure! I've never gotten to be so high up in the mountians before. I feel so lucky to have been able to explore and see the sites, I'd love to go back and see even more one day.

Alabama

My current residency! I love Huntsville and all the beauty that comes with it. Moving 700 miles was one of the best choices I've made to date. I hope to continue to travel and move around in the future.
